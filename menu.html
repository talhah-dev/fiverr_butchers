About Our Menu

At the Butchers of Llanddarog, we believe that great food begins with great provenance. That’s why we take pride in sourcing our ingredients locally and responsibly, ensuring quality you can taste in every bite. As you explore our menus, you’ll notice we’ve honoured the classics while also adding our own creative twist, offering something comforting yet refreshingly different. We’re passionate about what we serve and are always happy to accommodate any allergies or dietary requirements. Your enjoyment is at the heart of everything we do.
